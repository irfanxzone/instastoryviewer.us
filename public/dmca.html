DMCA Policy

Copyright infringement notices and content removal requests

Last updated: June 29, 2026, 10:02 AM PKT
Important: INSTAanonymous only displays publicly available Instagram content. We do not host, store, or re-publish Instagram media on our servers. If you believe content is being displayed improperly, please read this page carefully before submitting a notice.

1. Our DMCA Commitment

INSTAanonymous respects the intellectual property rights of content creators and rights holders. We comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) and other applicable copyright laws. We will respond expeditiously to valid notices of claimed copyright infringement.

2. How Our Service Works (Important Context)

INSTAanonymous does not host Instagram content on our servers. Instead, the Service:

  • Fetches publicly available content from Instagram's platform in real-time
  • Caches data temporarily (up to 24 hours) for performance reasons
  • Proxies images through our servers to overcome cross-origin restrictions

Because we only display publicly available Instagram content, the most direct way to have content removed is to set your Instagram account to Private or delete the specific post on Instagram. Our Service will automatically stop displaying content that is no longer publicly available on Instagram within 24 hours (cache expiry).

3. Filing a DMCA Takedown Notice

If you are a copyright owner or authorised agent and believe that content accessible through our Service infringes your copyright, you may submit a notice containing the following information as required by 17 U.S.C. § 512(c)(3):

  • Your identity: Your full legal name, company name (if applicable), and contact information (email, postal address, phone number)
  • Description of the copyrighted work: Identify the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed. If multiple works, a representative list.
  • Location of the infringing material: The specific URL(s) on our Service where the allegedly infringing material appears
  • Good faith belief statement: A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorised by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law
  • Accuracy statement: A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information in your notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorised to act on the copyright owner's behalf
  • Signature: Your physical or electronic signature

4. How to Submit Your Notice

Send your DMCA notice to our designated copyright agent via:

  • Email (preferred): instastoryviewer.us@gmail.com
  • Contact form: Contact Us page (select "DMCA / Content removal" as the subject)

We respond to all valid DMCA notices within 48 business hours. Content confirmed as infringing will be removed from our cache within 24 hours of verification.

5. Counter-Notification

If you believe content was removed in error or is not infringing, you may submit a counter-notification containing:

  • Your physical or electronic signature
  • Identification of the material that has been removed and its location before removal
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the material was removed by mistake or misidentification
  • Your name, address, phone number, and consent to jurisdiction of your local federal district court

6. Repeat Infringers

In accordance with the DMCA, we maintain a policy to terminate, in appropriate circumstances, the accounts and access of repeat copyright infringers.

7. Misuse of DMCA Process

Please note that under 17 U.S.C. § 512(f), any person who knowingly materially misrepresents that material is infringing may be liable for damages, including costs and attorneys' fees. We reserve the right to seek damages from those who submit fraudulent DMCA notices.

8. Contact

For DMCA notices or any copyright-related questions: instastoryviewer.us@gmail.com or use our Contact page.
