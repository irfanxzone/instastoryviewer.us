Privacy Policy

How we handle your data — clearly and honestly

Last updated: June 29, 2026, 10:02 AM PKT

1. Introduction

Welcome to INSTAanonymous ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website at instaanonymous.com (the "Service"). By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Information you do not provide: INSTAanonymous does not require you to create an account, register, or provide any personal information to use the Service.

Automatically collected data: When you visit our website, our servers may log standard technical information including:

  • Your IP address (used to enforce rate limits and prevent abuse)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on the Service
  • Date and time of your visit
  • Referring URL

This information is collected in server logs for security, abuse prevention, and analytics purposes only. We do not sell or share this data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Search queries: When you search for an Instagram username, that username is temporarily processed by our servers to fetch the relevant public data. Search queries are not stored permanently and are not linked to your identity.

3. Cookies

INSTAanonymous uses minimal cookies necessary for the Service to function. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies that follow you across websites. Specifically:

  • We may set a session cookie to manage rate limiting per user session.
  • We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar third-party tracking tools.

4. How We Use Information

We use the limited information collected to:

  • Operate and maintain the Service
  • Prevent abuse, spam, and excessive requests (rate limiting)
  • Monitor for security incidents
  • Improve the Service based on aggregate usage patterns

We do not use your information to serve personalised advertisements.

5. Instagram Data

Our Service fetches publicly available data from Instagram's public-facing interfaces. We only display data that Instagram makes publicly accessible without requiring a login for the specific profile. We do not:

  • Store fetched Instagram content permanently on our servers
  • Index or aggregate Instagram content for resale or distribution
  • Access private account content or bypass any Instagram restriction

Fetched data is cached temporarily (up to 15 minutes) for performance, then discarded.

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only:

  • With hosting or infrastructure providers necessary to operate the Service, under confidentiality obligations
  • If required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights
  • In connection with a business transfer (merger, acquisition), with notice provided to users

7. Data Retention

Server access logs are retained for a maximum of 30 days for security purposes, then deleted. Cached Instagram profile data is discarded after 24 hours at most. We do not maintain long-term databases of user activity or Instagram content.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, rate limiting, and server hardening. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal information we hold about you. Since we collect minimal data and do not link it to identities, such requests can typically be fulfilled by confirming we hold no profile-specific data about you. Contact us at our contact page for any data requests.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

12. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please reach us at: Contact Us or email instastoryviewer.us@gmail.com.
