How to Download Instagram Reels: Complete 2026 Guide

Step-by-step guide to saving any public Instagram reel for free without an app.

📅 June 30, 2026, 7:01 AM PKT · Tutorial

Why There's No Built-In Download Button

Instagram intentionally removes direct download options to keep users on-platform. However, downloading public reels for personal use is widely considered acceptable, and many tools make it straightforward.

How to Download Reels with INSTAanonymous

  • Step 1: Open INSTAanonymous and search for the username whose reels you want to save.
  • Step 2: Wait for the profile to load, then click the Reels tab.
  • Step 3: Click any reel card to open the full viewer. Click ⬇ Download.
  • Step 4: Your browser will save the .mp4 file directly to your Downloads folder.

What File Format Are Reels Saved As?

Reels are saved as standard MP4 video files, compatible with all major video players, phones, and editing software. No conversion needed.

Can I Download Reels from Private Accounts?

No. INSTAanonymous only supports public accounts. If an account is set to Private on Instagram, their reels are not accessible through our tool.

Are There Download Limits?

There are no limits on how many public reels you can download. However, repeated automated downloading is rate-limited to prevent server abuse. Normal human usage has no restrictions.
