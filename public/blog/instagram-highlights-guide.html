Instagram Highlights Explained: View & Save Them Easily

Learn what Instagram Highlights are, how to view them anonymously, and how to save highlight media.

📅 July 1, 2026, 6:42 AM PKT · Explainer

What Are Instagram Highlights?

Highlights are curated collections of past stories that a user chooses to keep permanently on their profile, below their bio. Unlike regular stories that disappear after 24 hours, highlights last indefinitely until the user removes them.

How to View Highlights Anonymously

  • Search for any public username on INSTAanonymous.
  • After the profile loads, click the Highlights tab.
  • Each highlight appears as a cover image with the title the user set (e.g. "Travel", "Food", "Work").
  • Click a highlight card to see the individual story items inside it.

Why Are Some Highlights Empty?

Instagram only exposes highlight metadata (cover image, title) through public APIs. Individual story items within highlights require additional API access that depends on whether the account's session context is available. If highlights show covers but no stories inside, this is a platform limitation, not a bug.

Saving Highlight Media

Click any highlight item thumbnail to open the viewer, then use the ⬇ Download button to save images or videos. This works for publicly accessible highlight items.
